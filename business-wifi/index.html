What is business WiFi?

What is business WiFi?

Business WiFi is a professionally designed wireless network built to support the demands of modern commercial environments. Unlike a domestic broadband router, business WiFi uses enterprise-grade equipment and multiple access points to deliver reliable, high-performance connectivity throughout your premises.

Designed for commercial environments, not domestic use
Multiple access points for whole-building coverage
Supports large numbers of simultaneous connections
Enterprise-grade performance and stability
Who needs it?

Any business depending on reliable connectivity — from offices and warehouses to care homes and retail premises.

Security & reliability

Network segregation, enterprise encryption and proactive monitoring keep your data safe and your connection stable.
